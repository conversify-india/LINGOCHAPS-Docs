Intake & Global Communications

Mission Critical: Intake Protocols

CRITICAL REQUIREMENT: INSTANT REPLY

Clients for YouTube projects operate via WhatsApp and Email. An Instant Reply is mandatory. Team members must acknowledge receipt within 2-5 minutes to maintain trust and engagement.

Timezone Audit

The Project Manager or Associate must check the client's timezone before accepting tasks. This ensures delivery slots are realistic for translators.

  • Align client deadline with translator active hours.
  • Confirm resource bandwidth before "Proceed" signal.
  • Communicate ETA only after resource confirmation.

Forwarding Protocol

All client communications must be instantly mirrored to the relevant internal whatsapp execution groups.

REQUIRED MESSAGES TO FORWARD:

  • ETA: Send internal deadline expectations immediately.
  • Proceed: Official green-light for translators.
  • Video Link: Direct raw links sent by the client.

Data Integrity

Allocation Sheet: Master Inventory Guide

Understanding the Columns

1. Operational Ownership

  • Task accepted by: The internal PM responsible for the task.
  • Date of Enrollment: When the project hit our system.
  • Assigned on: The source platform (e.g., Lumos).

2. Resource & Delivery

  • Translators: The specific linguist assigned to the ID.
  • Deadline: Strict cutoff times (typically 5:30 PM or 9:00 PM).
  • Total minutes done: Real-time completion tracking.

Asset Completion

Template Architecture & Completion

Required Data Fields

  • Header Info: Video Title, Link, Target Language, and Profile Name.
  • Video Summary: A concise 2-3 line overview of the content.
  • Metadata: Translated Video Description and Thumbnail Text.
  • Additional Comments: Notes on specific terminology or nuances.

Execution Rules

1. Timestamping: Must follow (HH:MM:SS) format per minute.

2. Cultural Adaptation: Translate idioms/slang for context, not literally. Provide English equivalent in brackets if needed.

3. Speaker ID: Clearly identify Speaker A, B, etc., as they speak.

4. Manual Only: Absolutely NO Machine Translation allowed.

Quality Assurance

Review Protocols & Quality Control

P1

Phase 1: Structural Audit

Technical verification of metadata and assets.

  • OST Verification: Check Original Script/Text against source audio.
  • Metadata Check: Verify titles, tags, and description formatting.
  • Language ID: Confirm correct dialect and regional markers.
P2

Phase 2: Linguistic Depth

Human review of sync, tone, and nuances.

Active Audio Listening: Full speech path review for sync and natural flow.

Review Notes: Annotate specific timestamps for any suggested edits.

GOAL: ZERO DEFECT HANDOVER

Final approval for linguistic integrity and formatting excellence.
