# 📖 Step-by-Step Project Creation Manual

Follow these exact steps every time you start a new translation project at Lingo Chaps.

---

### Step 1: Initialize the Project
1.  Click the **Projects** icon in the top left sidebar.
2.  Click the blue **`+ New Project`** button.
3.  **Project Title:** Enter the Client Name + Language Pair (e.g., *Nike - English to Spanish*).
4.  **Project Owner:** Set this to yourself or the project manager.
5.  **Project Group:** Select the client's name (or "Add New Group" if it's a new client).
6.  Click **Add**.

### Step 2: Import the Workflow
1.  Inside your new project, click the **Tasks** tab.
2.  Click the **small down-arrow `v`** on the right side of the blue **"Add Task"** button.
3.  Select **`Add from Template`**.
4.  Choose **`Standard TEP (Trans-Edit-QC)`**.
5.  Click **Add**. 
    *   *Result: Three tasks (Translation, Editing, Final QC) will appear with pre-set durations.*

### Step 3: Assign and Configure (The "Data" Step)
Click on each task one by one and fill in the **Custom Fields** at the bottom:
1.  **Owner:** Assign to the freelancer (or Sonali/yourself).
2.  **Source & Target Language:** Select from the dropdowns.
3.  **Upwork Contract URL:** Paste the link to the active Upwork contract.
4.  **Upwork Freelancer Cost:** Enter the USD amount we are paying for this specific task.

### Step 4: Daily Management
*   As the freelancer works, move the status to **`In Translation`**.
*   When they finish, move it to **`Review in Progress`** and assign the next task to the Editor.

### Step 5: Final Delivery (Automated)
Once the Final QC is complete and the files are uploaded to Zoho:
1.  Open the **`03. Final QC & Delivery`** task.
2.  Change the status to **`Awaiting Client Approval`**.
3.  **Click Save.**
    *   *Result: The client will instantly receive the professional delivery email!*

---

### Step 6: Handling Revisions (If needed)
If the client asks for changes:
1.  Go to the **Revision Requests** module in the sidebar.
2.  Click **`+ New Revision Request`**.
3.  Select the **`Revision Type`** (e.g., Grammar or Style).
4.  Assign it back to the freelancer to fix.

---

**Summary:** This manual ensures that every project at Lingo Chaps follows the same high standard of quality and financial tracking!
