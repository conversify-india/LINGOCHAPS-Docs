## 🛠 Core Operational Protocols

To maintain our 100% acceptance rate and client trust, all team members must adhere to these "Non-Negotiable" standards:

### 1. Zero-Delay Maintenance
The **Allocation Sheet** and **Template.docx** must be updated in real-time. We do not wait for project completion to log milestones.

### 2. The 5-Minute Rule (WhatsApp/Email)
Client intake requests require an **Instant Reply** within 2-5 minutes to confirm receipt and verify resource bandwidth.

### 3. Anti-MT Policy (Zero Tolerance)
Absolutely no Machine Translation (Google Translate/DeepL) is permitted. We deliver 100% human-verified, culturally adapted content.
