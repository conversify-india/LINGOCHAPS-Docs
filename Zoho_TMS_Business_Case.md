# 📈 Business Case: Why we moved to Zoho TMS

This document outlines the strategic value of the new system for stakeholders and clients.

---

## The Problem (Before Zoho)
*   **Scattered Data:** Information was spread across Upwork, WhatsApp, and spreadsheets.
*   **Manual Delivery:** Emails were sent manually, leading to delays and inconsistent formatting.
*   **Invisible Profits:** It was difficult to calculate real-time profit margins for individual projects.
*   **Feedback Loops:** Revision requests were lost in long email chains.

## The Solution (The Zoho TMS)
*   **Centralization:** Every Upwork URL, cost, and file is tied to a single Project ID.
*   **Automated Delivery:** A professional "One-Click" system that alerts clients the moment work is ready.
*   **Quality Analytics:** Categorizing revisions allows us to see which freelancers are underperforming.
*   **Standardization:** The TEP (Trans-Edit-QC) cycle is now baked into every project, ensuring nothing is skipped.

## Future Benefits
1.  **Freelancer Scorecards:** We can eventually generate reports on which freelancers have the lowest revision rates.
2.  **Financial Reporting:** Monthly reports will show our total "Upwork Spend" vs. "Client Revenue" automatically.
3.  **Client Confidence:** Clients are more likely to stay with an agency that provides a professional, structured portal for their work.
