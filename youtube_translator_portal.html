Online Execution Only

Work directly on the Google Doc link. Offline work is not acceptable.

02

Comprehensive Coverage

Translate Title, Description, Thumbnail text, and all Onscreen text (rolling or fixed).

03

Video Summary

Write a concise 2-3 line summary explaining the video content at the top of the doc.

04

Comment Resolution

Monitor and resolve all internal comments or queries added to the file.

05

Verbatim & No Masking

Translate everything verbatim. No masking of abusive words or swears. Translate exactly as spoken.

Technical Timestamps

Mark inaudible parts or cut speech with timestamps. Mark foreign languages if not understood (e.g., Arabic: 0:00-0:16).

07

Speaker Labeling

Label all speakers by name or appearance (e.g., Man in Black Coat, Lady in Red Dress).

08

Cultural Nuance

Provide notes for cultural references, metaphors, slurs, slang, idioms, and similes literally.
